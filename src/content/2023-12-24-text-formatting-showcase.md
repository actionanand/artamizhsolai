## Basic Text Styles

### Bold Text

Make your text stand out with ::bold{bold formatting}. This is perfect for emphasizing key points.

**Example usage:**
- The ::bold{most important} concept is understanding the basics.
- Remember to ::bold{save your work} frequently.
- ::bold{Never} share your password with anyone.

### Italic Text

Use ::italic{italic text} to add subtle emphasis or indicate titles and foreign words.

**Example usage:**
- The term ::italic{metadata} refers to data about data.
- She said, ::italic{Let's make it happen!}
- The book ::italic{The Art of Code} is a must-read.

## Accessibility Tips

::combo[bg:#e8f5e9;color:#2e7d32;weight:bold]{â™¿ Accessibility Matters}

Always consider users with visual impairments:

1. ::bold{Contrast}: Ensure text has at least 4.5:1 contrast ratio
2. ::bold{Redundancy}: Don't rely on color alone - use icons and text
3. ::bold{Readability}: Avoid excessive formatting that makes text hard to read
4. ::bold{Testing}: Preview your content with different color blindness filters
